Easily share your guide with anyone and invite them to edit or view, keeping your team aligned and your content up to date. Let’s see how.
Go to stg.guidde.com
01
Share from Homepage
From your homepage, click the three dots on your guide, then select “Share” from the opened menu.
02
Share from View Page
Or share from the video's view page, on the upper right.
03
Share Modal
In the opened menu, you can add viewers or invite someone to edit your guide.
04
Add People with Aceess
Start typing a members name. If they’re in your workspace, the auto-suggest will show them. Click their name to select.
05
Open Permission Settings
In the permission dropdown on the right, you canset the user’s access level.
06
Set Access
As a viewer or editor
07
Viewer
Viewers can watch your guide, unless their workspace role gives them editing rights.
08
Editor
Editors can make changes, even if they’re not content-managers on your plan.
09
Add a Message
You can add a brief message if you like
10
Share Invitation
And click Share once you're ready.
11
Email Notification
Your collaborator will receive an email invitation
12
Click Open Guidde
And they can simply click the link to access the guide
13
Admins can Add External Users
Admins can also invite collaborators outside of your workspace to view or edit a guide.
14
Add Email and Click Enter
Once you’ve entered an email, click the input field and press Enter.
15
Select Access Permission
A menu will appear where you can set the invite access to viewer or editor.
16
Add a Message
Personalize the invitationwith a message if you like!
17
Click Share
And click "Share"
18
Confirm Sharing Outside Your Workspace
A popup will ask if you want to share access outside your workspace. Click “Share Anyway” to confirm.
19
Invite to Edit - Email
Your collaborator will receive an email invitation. They can simply click Open guide to access it.
20
View or Edit the Guidde
The guide opens in a new tab, where they can watch the video or access the editor.
21
Edit the Guidde
In the video editor, they can make the edits they wish.
22
Click Done
And click done when finished, to finalize the guide.
23
Your guidde is ready
Your guide will be saved with the changes, along with a step-by-step article below.
24
Review People with Access
You can always see who has access to a guide by clicking share, under People with access.
25
Click for More Info
Click into any profile image to see each added user's access level
26
Change / Remove Access
And change or remove it as needed.
27
Pro Tip: Use Version History!
For a detailed view of who edited a guidde, on the enterprise plan, you can click the three dots at the top right in the video editor, and access version history.
28
version history
Here, you’ll see a detailed history of your guide, including who published each version and when, making it easy for creators to keep track.
That’s it! Inviting others to edit makes it easy to share your guides and collaborate with anyone who needs access, so your content stays accurate, aligned, and up to date. Give it a try!
